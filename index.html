Live Freely With No Boundaries

Plan a perfect day with everywhere Canada has to offer. Explore any free public / no permit required place. Enjoy parks and sanctuaries during your visit.

About this Website

This website was created not only for a class project, but also because of the drastic differences between the United States and Canada when asking 'Where am I allowed to be?' These areas are places that anyone can visit for free with no permit required. Some areas, however, do need a reservation to be filled out before your visit.

At the moment the only data files are:

  • Saskatchewan National and Provincial Parks
  • Manitoba National and Provincial Parks
  • Ontario Protected Areas and Provincial Parks
  • Quebec Aquatic and Biodiversity Protected Areas, Migratory Bird Sanctuaries, and National and Provincial Parks
    • -Quebec Migratory Bird Sanctuaries are not nesscessarily open access, but are still cool
  • Prince Edward Island Provincial Parks and Wildlife Management Areas
  • New Brunswick Wildlife Provincial Parks, Refuge and Management Areas
  • Nova Scotia National and Provincial Parks
    • -Provincial Parks only sometimes show up
  • Newfoundland and Labrador, Alberta, British Columbia, Northwest Territories, Nunavut, and Yukon National Parks
    • -The National Parks tab on the map does not contain all parks.

More data files will be added soon as well as data adjustments to the ones viewable. Some provincial tabs include National Parks that may or may not be already on the map.
